Bio:

Hello my name is Jason Lee and as a multimedia designer who specializes in project management, I am comfortable working in large groups or collaborating on specialized projects. Being an experienced communicator and manager, I have extensive background in conflict resolution, time management, and customer relations.

I am a graphic designer who appreciates the aesthetic appeal of art in virtual space! When I was in high school in the 90's I was interested in many forms of art, both technical and creative. Wanting to try something different I began studying computers to integrate my artistic expression. I was hooked from the very first class and I have been graphic designing ever since.

During my time at Washington State University Vanncouver, I have had the pleasure of being apart of a unique and creative program called the Creative Media and Digital Culture (CMDC) directed by Dr. Dene Grigar. Working on my Bachelor of Art's in Digital Technology and Culture (DTC) with a minor in English, I have gained valuable work experience in project management, web development, animation, and design.

I enjoy editing video and audio, or animating in 2D & 3D using Maya and Adobe AfterEffects in my spare time. Combining these techniques, I have been working on projects to expand my knowledge and skill using augmented reality.

Currently, I am working with two other students from WSUV to launch our own Augmented T-shirt company called: Trinequitis.

So Please..., stay tuned for more exciting work to come in the future. I promise to update the links with pics and cool animation as I continue to grow! Feel free to follow me on any social media site to stay tuned in.
