As an animator who specializes in both 2D and 3D using Adobe After Effects and Maya, I have the ability to create movies, short videos, animations, infographics, and documentaries.

Combining my communication skills and the classes I have taken at Washington State University Vancouver, I am comfortable in video production and interviews.
