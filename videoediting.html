When does gaming become an addiction?

Focusing on real world addictions as a communications project of mine, I interviewed local professionals and video documented someone with a troubling addiction to highlight the importance of this crisis in our community.

Curious due to a family member who seemed to struggle with addiction, I researched and synthesized information to present to the public as a resource.

Using Adobe Premier, I compiled my video interviews and edited my sound clips to sync with the video.
